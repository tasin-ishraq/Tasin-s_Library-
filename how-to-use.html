📖 How to Use

Learn how to navigate and enjoy Tasin's Digital Library

🏠 Getting Started

Welcome to Tasin's Digital Library! Here's how to get started:

  • Click "Browse Library" on the home page to explore our collection
  • You'll see a list of all available writers and authors
  • Books are organized by writers and categories
💡 Tip: All books in our library are completely free to read and download!
👥 Browsing Writers

Explore books organized by their authors:

  • View all available writers on the Writers page
  • Each writer has an icon to help you recognize them quickly
  • For special writers, you'll see their books organized by categories (like Humayun Ahmed's categories: Himu, Misir Ali, etc.)
  • For other writers, all books are displayed together
  • Click on any book spine to view more details and download options
📚 Exploring Categories

For writers with categories (like Humayun Ahmed, Zafor Iqbal, Satyajit Roy):

  • You'll see category cards instead of individual books on the Writers page
  • Each category shows the number of books available
  • Click any category card to see all books in that collection
  • Each book is displayed as a colorful spine for an authentic shelf appearance
📖 Reading & Downloading Books

Once you've selected a book:

  • Click the "Read Book" button to view it online in your browser
  • Click the "Download" button to save the PDF to your device
  • Downloaded books can be read offline anytime without internet
  • The file remains on your device until you delete it
📱 Multi-Device: Read on your phone, tablet, or computer - your library follows you everywhere!

🔍 Quick Search

Find books instantly on the Writers page:

  • Use the search box at the top of the Writers page
  • Search by writer name to find all their works
  • Search by book title to quickly locate specific books
  • Search by category name to discover themed collections
  • Results update instantly as you type
  • Click any search result to jump directly to that writer or book
  • Use the Clear button to reset your search
⚡ Speed Tip: The search is real-time, so just start typing to find what you're looking for instantly!
❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are all books really free?

A: Yes! Tasin's Digital Library offers 100% free access to all books. No subscriptions or hidden charges.

Q: Can I read books offline?

A: Absolutely! Download any book as a PDF and read it anytime without needing internet.

Q: What formats are available?

A: All books are available in PDF format, which works on any device with a PDF reader.

Q: How many books are in the library?

A: We have 500+ books from 50+ authors and growing! Check the homepage for the latest count.
